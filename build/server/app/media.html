Ervin Laszlo

is a Hungarian philosopher of science, systems theorist, integral theorist (and originally a concert pianist) who has published about 75 books and over 400 papers. An advocate of the theory of quantum consciousness, Laszlo has a PhD from the Sorbonne and is the recipient of four honorary PhDs (from the United States, Canada, Finland, and Hungary).

Chavalit Tsao

is the fourth-generation leader of IMC Pan Asia Alliance Group. Born in the East and educated in the West, he became chairman in 1995 and transformed the family business from a traditional shipping company to a multinational conglomerate.

Nóra Csiszár
